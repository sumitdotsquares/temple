Temple University is a stop on many local public transportation routes.

Broad Street Subway

All local Broad Street subway trains (the orange line) stop at Cecil B. Moore Station (Broad and Cecil B. Moore Avenue).

By SEPTA Bus

The "4" and "16" buses stop on Broad Street at Cecil B. Moore Avenue, Montgomery Avenue, Polett Walk and Norris Street.

The "23" bus stops on 12th Street (southbound) and 11th Street (northbound) at Polett Walk, Montgomery Avenue and Cecil B. Moore Avenue.

SEPTA Regional Rail Lines

All lines stop at Temple University Station, located at 10th and Berks Streets. Consult your train schedule for times.

For more information, please visit SEPTA's website: www.septa.org.
