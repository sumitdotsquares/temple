Temple University is located in the city of Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. Philadelphia is the fifth-largest city in the country and is famous as the birthplace of freedom. The city has many American historical landmarks, including Independence Hall, the Liberty Bell, the Betsy Ross House, and more. Philadelphia offers cultural, culinary, artistic and ethnic treasures at every turn.

The Philadelphia International Airport is a big airport and located only a few miles from the University. For first time visitrs, we recommend that you take a taxi cab to your destination into Center City and/or TMI. Nearby airports includes the JFK Airport (New York) and Newark Airport (New Jersey).

Temple University's Main Campus is very accessible whether you are driving yourself, or taking any of the various forms of public transit available in the city.
