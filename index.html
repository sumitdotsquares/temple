Mathematics at the crossroads of discovery, innovation and education

The Department of Mathematics at Temple University is home to a broad spectrum of theoretical and applied mathematical research by world-class faculty, and is strategically positioned to provide, strengthen and support mathematical education at all levels within the University.

The department also serves as an ambassador of our profession to the community at large, providing visibility of the fundamental role played by mathematics in today's scientific and technological advances and promoting excellence in the education of the next generation of STEM leaders.
