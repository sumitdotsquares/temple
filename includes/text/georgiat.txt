
Georgia Triantafillou is interested in the interplay between Algebraic Topology and Geometric Topology and in particular in developing algebraic models that increasingly capture more refined geometric properties of spaces.
 
Undergraduate Diploma from the University of Athens, Greece.  Ph.D. in Mathematics from the University of Bonn, Germany.
  
Academic positions in the past: Institute of Advanced Study at Princeton, University of Chicago, University of Minnesota, University of Crete, Greece, University of Athens.
