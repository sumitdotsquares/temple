      M. Stover wins Dean's Distinguished Research Award.

      • March, 2024

        Irina Mitrea receives University Research Award.

        The award, the universities highest recognition for research achievement, cites Mitrea's many contributions to partial differential equations and harmonic analysis through her career and highlights the completion of her recent five volume monograph Geometric Functional Analysis. 


        • March, 2024

          Sonia Kovalevsky Day returns to campus.

          The department is pleased to announce that we will hold the tenth annual Sonia Kovalevsky Math Day for Girls at Temple University on Saturday, April 13th, 10am--2pm.
           
          The event is free and open to all female-identifying students currently in grades 5 through 8.
           
          Registration and information are online at:
           
          https://math.temple.edu/events/conferences/skday/
